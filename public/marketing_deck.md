# Pillar: Construction Management Evolved
---
## 1. The Challenge: Why Construction is Hard
Contractors today face a "Triad of Chaos":
*   **Fragmented Data**: Spreadsheets for expenses, emails for communication, and mental notes for inventory. Nothing talks to anything else.
*   **Client Anxiety**: Clients feel in the dark about where their money is going, leading to mistrust and constant "status update" calls.
*   **Financial Blindspots**: Without real-time tracking, profit margins bleed out through unbilled change orders, lost tools, and forgotten material costs.
---
## 2. The Solution: Pillar
**Pillar is the all-in-one operating system for the modern contractor.**
It unifies project management, financial tracking, and client communication into a single, beautiful platform. We replace chaos with clarity, ensuring every dollar is accounted for and every client feels secure.
---
## 3. Core Capabilities
### Project Command Center
*   **Centralized Dashboard**: See active projects, upcoming deadlines, and critical alerts in one view.
*   **Visual Timeline**: Plan projects with a drag-and-drop Gantt chart. Track "Plan vs. Actual" progress in real-time.
*   **Task Management**: Assign tasks to personnel or sub-contractors and track them to completion.
### Financial Intelligence
*   **Live BOM Tracking**: Log expenses against a Bill of Materials instantly.
*   **Automated Fees**: Calculate contractor fees (Fixed or Percentage) automatically as costs accrue.
*   **Profit & Loss**: View real-time P&L statements per project and for the entire business.
### Client Portal
*   **Transparency**: Give clients a secure, read-only link to view their project's financial status and progress photos.
*   **Trust**: Eliminate "where is my money?" questions with clear, visual fund utilization graphs.
*   **Collaboration**: Clients can approve Change Orders and upload documents directly through the portal.
### Resource Logistics
*   **Inventory Management**: Track tools and workshop materials. Know exactly what is on-site and what is in the warehouse.
*   **Personnel Tracking**: Manage staff, track days off, and assign crews to specific project sites.
*   **Supplier Database**: Maintain a directory of vendors and material price lists for quick quoting.
---
## 4. Financial Mastery
Pillar turns contractors into financial experts without the accounting degree.
*   **Smart Invoicing**: Generate professional invoices in seconds. Pull in unbilled expenses, material usage, and approved change orders with one click.
*   **Change Order Management**: Create formal quotes for scope changes. Get client approval digitally before work begins.
*   **Real-Time Ledger**: Every deposit and expense is automatically recorded in a double-entry style ledger for audit-ready books.

## 5. Operational Efficiency
